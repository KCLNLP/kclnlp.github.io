Research Themes
Addressing LLM limitations
Approaches for addressing LLM limitations, such as lack of robust reasoning, difficult to control their outputs, and hallucination.
Narrative Understanding with LLMs
Comprehend extended and complex texts, such as novels, ESG reports and long articles.
Trustworthy AI
Interpreting neural model decisions.
AI for Education
AI-enabled explainable student answer assessment, personalised feedback for secondary school science education.
NLP Application
NLP for healthcare, finance, education, and social media.
Multimodal learning
Analysing multimodal data such as text and image for story illustration, caption generation and image generation.
